About Us

Sole Mission: Reviving Every Step

“Because Every Step Deserves a Clean Slate”. Sole Mission was born out of a practical observation and a touch of laziness. The founders noticed that people would go to great lengths to clean even their carpets, so why not their shoes? This insight inspired them to create a niche in specialized shoe cleaning and restoration services.

The concept was initially sparked by the founders’ previous partners, while one of the key members focused on the marketing and innovation side. They started with basic cleaning services in 2021, but as the business grew, so did their expertise. One of the founders took it upon themselves to learn the intricacies of shoe repainting and reglueing, ensuring they could offer comprehensive solutions to their clients.

Still running strong to this day, Sole Mission provides a wide range of services, from deep cleaning and stain removal to color restoration, damage repair, sole reglueing, and material refreshment. With meticulous attention to detail, they strive to make each pair of shoes look as good as new, extending their lifespan and preserving their value.
